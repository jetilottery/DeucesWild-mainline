DEUCES WILD

Let the glimmering gold of Deuces Wild bring you riches! Win prizes by revealing Wild 2 symbols. Reveal double Wild 2 symbols to double the prize and triple Wild 2 symbols to triple the prize! When 3 or more special Joker Hat symbols are revealed, the bonus game is triggered, giving you a chance to spin the wheel and win even more glittering prizes!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the minus sign (-) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the plus sign (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Press each Card Suit symbol to reveal Wild 2 symbols, Card symbols, or Joker Hat symbols. Reveal any Wild 2 symbols and win the corresponding prize. Find a double Wild 2 symbol and double the corresponding prize, and a triple Wild 2 symbol to triple the corresponding prize.

Reveal 3 or more Joker Hat symbols to trigger the bonus game. Spin the wheel for a chance to win more prizes. Win a prize when the pointer lands on a prize value. Prizes accumulate until the bonus ends. Advance to the next wheel with richer prizes when the pointer lands on an Arrow symbol. Landing on a black X symbol removes one Joker Hat symbol. The bonus game ends when no more Joker Hat symbols remain.

Dismiss the win message and view the game's outcome by pressing the message.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made and any prizes have been awarded. If a Bonus is triggered, make a selection and press REVEAL ALL to play the rest of the Bonus game to completion automatically.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.

Wins are shown in currency

In this game, the wheel is a graphical animation for entertainment purposes only and does not represent an actual wheel. The chances of obtaining a specific segment are therefore not equiprobable.
